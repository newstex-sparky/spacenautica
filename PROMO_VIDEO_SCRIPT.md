## SOUND DESIGN GUIDE

### **Music**
- **Genre:** Electronic / Sci-Fi / Epic
- **Tempo:** 120–140 BPM
- **Instruments:** Synthesizers, deep bass, dramatic pads

### **Sound Effects**
- **Mining Lasers:** High-pitched zaps
- **Particle Explosions:** Swish + sparkle
- **O2 Refill:** Whoosh + power-up sound
- **O2 Depletion:** Slow heartbeat
- **Music Build-up:** Crescendos

---

## VIDEO EDIT TIPS

### **Color Grading**
- **Space scenes:** Deep blues, purples, blacks
- **Asteroids:** Grays, blues, reds (resource colors)
- **O2 Bar:** Gradients (green → yellow → red)

### **Transitions**
- **Cuts:** Sharp, quick
- **Fades:** Dramatic fades between shots
- **Zooms:** Dynamic zooms for impact

### **Speed**
- **Editing style:** Fast, energetic
- **Scene pacing:** 2–3 seconds per shot
- **Montages:** 4–6 shots/second
